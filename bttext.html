NOUNS
A nounrepresents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper:It is the name of a particular object,whether person,place,animal,idea or thing.

Common:It is the name possessed by things in general.

Collective:It is the name repressenting a group of things.

Abstract:It is the name representing qualities,emotions,conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

Apronounis a word used in place of a noun.A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:

Personal pronoun:It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronouns:It stands as the answer to the question about a person,place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to a person,thing or a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns:It relates to a noun,pronoun,word or expression preceding it in the sentence,and at the same time connects two statement.

Demonstrative pronoun:A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.

Reflexive pronoun:It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
